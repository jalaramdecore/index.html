About Jalaram Decor

Cabinet Makers & Interior Experts

Jalaram Decor is a trusted name in modern interior and cabinet solutions. We specialize in premium modular kitchens, wardrobes, TV units, aluminium profile systems, glazing shutters, storage drawers, and customized furniture solutions.

Under the leadership of Kanak Purushottam Limbda, we focus on quality workmanship, elegant finishing, durable materials, and customer satisfaction.

We understand every client’s imagination and convert ideas into practical, stylish, and comfortable living spaces.

From residential interiors to commercial decorative solutions, we deliver complete interior craftsmanship with perfection.
